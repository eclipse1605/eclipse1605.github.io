## 15th May - The first meeting

Today was our first meeting. I got the chance to speak with Yann, Osvaldo, and two other students from UCL who will also be joining the project over the summer: Sameer and Patrick.

We discussed the overall direction and workflow of the project, and decided to initially follow the timeline proposed in my proposal while remaining flexible and adapting based on the progress we observe along the way.

Since this project explores relatively uncharted territory, we agreed that the first major milestone should be successfully sampling from the correct posteriors.

After the meeting, I continued reading about and strengthening my understanding of WSGs (Wasserstein Gradient Flows).
